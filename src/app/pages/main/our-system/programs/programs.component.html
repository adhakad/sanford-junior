OUR PROGRAMS

We believe that age-appropriate handling of a child is the key to right upbringing. Following the same, SANFORD offers specialized age-wise classification of the tiny-tots.

SAN TODDLER (2.5 YEARS)

SAN Toddler Program is based on the concept of fun and learn, children are allowed to play and explore new things under the care and guidance of our teachers.

They are given scientifically planned age appropriate activities, which help them in:

Developing listening and language skills

Emotional, physical and social engagement

Developing a grasp hold to enable scribbling

Exploration of letters, Numbers, primary shapes and colors

Development of fine and gross motor skills

Cognitive development through sensorial activities

Learning by exploration

Encouraging curiosity

Developing vocabulary and communication skills

Fostering the seeds of self-reliance and team work

Numeracy & Literacy Readiness

To promote Self-help activities

SAN LEARNER (3 - 4 YEARS)

SAN Learner program is designed to help children in smooth transition between their elementary knowledge they gained during Toddler program and more structured and advanced SAN Learner curriculum.

The SAN Learner program focuses on building on

Personal, Social and Emotional Development

Enhancement of Cognitive Skills

Developing Numeracy, linguistic and Literacy skills

Inculcating Life-long learning skills

Development of aesthetic, imaginative and inquisitiveness senses.

Developing creative and constructive skills

Exploring and encouraging inbuilt talents

Improving the pincer grip of children for better holding of things, specially handwriting

SAN JUNIOR (4 - 5)

SAN Toddler Program is based on the concept of fun and learn, children are allowed to play and explore new things under the care and guidance of our teachers.

We encourage children to express themselves through Dance, Drama, Music, Art and Role Plays.The SAN Juniorprogram focuses on:

Promoting reading, writing and speaking and listening skills

Creating the road for Critical thinking

Targeting the holistic development of the child

Promoting self-confidence

Problem Solving Skills And Cognitive Development

Addressing psychological changes fitting them well with societal norms

Preparing for primary school

SAN SENIOR (5 - 6)

SAN Seniorprogram is aligned with State, CBSE, ICSE, Cambridge and IB school curriculum to give children a strong foundation for academic excellence that will be required to confidently enter the formal school setup.

Our SAN Seniorprogram focuses on:

Physical, Personal, Social, Emotional Skills

Literature And Language Skills

Creative And Expressive Skills

Language and Math skills

Logical Reasoning Skills

Exposure to Technology

Understanding of the World

Dance, Music, Drama & Confidence Building

Field Trips & Educational Tours
