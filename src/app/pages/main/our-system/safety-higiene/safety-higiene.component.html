SAFETY & HYGIENE

SANFORD is committed to give a safe, secure and hygienic environment to its students. We maintain highest level of safety and hygiene and follow all the Covid-19 Protocols to give our children a safe and hygienic environment. Deep cleaning and sanitization of classrooms, washrooms and sanitization of toys, play equipment, furniture, etc. is at regular intervals is our routine feature.

Every corner of the school remains under CCTV surveillance and a trained & attentive security guard is always on duty at the school gate. No visitor is allowed to go beyond the reception till the time children are there in the school.
