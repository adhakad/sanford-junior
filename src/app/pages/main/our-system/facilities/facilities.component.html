FACILITIES AT SANFORD

CLASSROOM

At SANFORD classrooms are a cozy, comfy, cuddly place where Children are constructively engaged and teachers are observing, facilitating, and motivating them. Our classrooms are thoughtfully designed and structured, keeping in mind the safety and wellbeing of children. We believe that small group activities can happen naturally in the classrooms hence, we keep 1:15 teacher to student ratio in our schools.

STAGE PRESENTATIONS

We desire to bring out the best in our little SANFORDees and make them smart and confidant individual. We motivate our children to show their talent on the stage in the form of dance, drama, recitation, music and singing, etc. It helps children to bring out their unique talent and help in psychological and personality development of the children.

PLAY AREA

Physical activities are very important for healthy development of the brain moreover it helps in the social, emotional, and cognitive development of the child. Therefore, we have fun-filled and child friendly play area in our schools well equipped with swings, slides, bouncy, tricycles, cars, trampoline, splash pool, etc.

MID-DAY MEALS

We provide healthy and nutritious meal to our children. During their meal times our little SANFORDees learn how to make nutritious choices, also discover a wide variety of different foods and develop healthy eating habits. The meals include one seasonal fruit and a healthy food like Kheer, Vermicelli, Pulao, Poha, Sandwich, etc.
