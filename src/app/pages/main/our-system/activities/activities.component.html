ACTIVITIES AT SANFORD

CELEBRATIONS & WORKSHOPS

The school celebrates various festivals, functions, special days, and other events like: Lohri, BasantPanchami, Baisakhi, Holi, RakshaBandhan, Janamashtami, Dussehra, Diwali, Gurupurab, Republic Day, Independence Day, Teacher’s Day, Mother’s Day, Earth Day, Hugs Day, Father’s Day, Daughter’s Day, Children’s Day, Friendship Day, Valentine’s Day, Grandparent’s Day, Earth Day, Sports Day, Annual Day (Cultural Program – Udaan), etc. The school always invites parents & grandparents in all the celebrations and events to participate & co-operate.

School Organise workshops and seminars for parents on positive parenting, hygiene & nutrition, orientation programme for the nursery school admission and on many other topics which are useful to parents in the upbringing of their children.

YOGA & Exercises

At Sanford World School, yoga and PT activities amidst nature are routinely conducted in intriguing and innovative ways, fostering a deep connection with the natural world. This unique approach not only allows students to revitalize themselves but also encourages a sense of mindfulness and appreciation for the environment.

SPORTS & GAMES

At Sanford World School, additional activities dedicated solely to sports are also organized. These activities help in maintaining the physical and mental well-being of students while also improving teamwork and discipline. Sports encourage students to work together, develop leadership skills, and enhance their ability to play as part of a team. Furthermore, they instill values of discipline, dedication, and enthusiasm among students.

COMPETITIONS

The School organise various academic, cultural and sports competitions for students in the school such as rhyme & recitation competition, colouring competition, Dance Competition, Fancy Dress Competition, age appropriate sports competitions and we prepare and motivate them to participate in inter-school competitions also.

EXTRA CURRICULAR ACTIVITIES

At Sanford World School, similar additional educational activities and sports are also explored, which keep students engaged and aid in their social, physical, and spiritual development. These activities may include sports, arts, music, dance, drama, science, technology, and other fields. All these programs provide students with a platform to develop new skills, enhance their critical thinking abilities, and offer them opportunities to gain new and interesting experiences through their exploration.
