GENERAL INFORMATION

SANFORD ALMANAC

The Almanac is the complete guide for school practices and regulations, having details regarding school fee, annual calendar, etc. and is given to each student. Parents are advised to see the almanac on regular basis as it may work as the effective mode of communication between the parents and the school.

UNIFORM

In order to create the sense of equality and discipline amongst children SANFORD students comes in specially designed colorful uniform. It also gives them unique identification. School uniform is available in the school.

SCHOOL TRANSPORT

The school provides transport facility to children. Though adequate measures are taken to ensure safety of the children. The school is not responsible for any mishap or injury, which may occur in and around the school or in the transport.

CHANGE OF ADDRESS

The school provides transport facility to children. Though adequate measures are taken to ensure safety of the children. The school is not responsible for any mishap or injury, which may occur in and around the school or in the transports.

WITHDRAWAL

One month written notice is required to withdraw the child from the school and on withdrawal no amount would be refunded in any case.

TRANSFER POLICY

As per the standard policies of SANFORD if a child is getting transferred from one branch to another branch in that situation both the branches have to follow the certain necessary steps, which are as follows:

1. A transfer application has to be given to both transferors as well as transferee branch 15 days prior to the transfer.

2. Mid-term transfers involve the sharing of information & documentation from one branch to another through the coordinators.

3. In case of transfer of student from one branch to another branch of SANFORD, the Annual Charges, Activity Charges, Tuition Fee and other charges of the new branch will be applicable.

4. Parents have to pay the tuition fee& other charges as applicable in the new branch.
