About Us

Sanford Junior is part of Sanford Education Group, India's leading Early Childhood & K-12 Education group backed by KKR Capital. Lighthouse Learning is committed to deliver holistic development of its 25000+ students across the network. The group works towards delivering a robust foundation for future generations with innovation in pedagogy, use of appropriate technology and focus on child safety. Celebrating 20+ years since inception, the group has always stayed ahead of the curve and is a harbinger of change in the educational landscape. With a ‘Child First’ ideology at its core, it has set new standards and raised the bar in many aspects, including enriching pedagogy and building a nurturing learning environment for children, using technology in enhancing learning engagements, creating employment and entrepreneurship opportunities for women or leading the way in enabling safety protocols for its students at the pre-school and schools.

Our Story

Since the launch of our first pre-school in 2016, Sanford Junior has grown into one of India’s most trusted and well-regarded education brands.

2016

Pioneering High-Quality Early Education

We launched our first two preschools in Mumbai with an unwavering commitment to early childhood education.

2017

Shaping Structured Kindergarten Learning

Responding to the demand for structured learning, we expanded with Sanford Junior and Sanford Senior, integrating our effective methodologies.

2018

Learning Evolved to: ‘Learning by Doing'

Elevated our curriculum with the "Learning by Doing" methodology, a dynamic approach to active learning.

2019

Igniting Curiosity through Learning by Inquiry

Enhanced our 'Learning by Doing' approach by introducing Learning by Inquiry, empowering curious minds through questioning.

2020

Safety and Learning United

Introduced the 'Child First, Safety First' Ideology. Honored as an Indian Power Brand and India's Most Trusted Brand.

2021

Nurturing Executive Function Skills

Enhanced curriculum focusing on 'Executive Function Skills,' vital for behaviour regulation and academic success.

2022

Tech-Enhanced Learning

Revolutionized early education with technology and earned the title of India's Best Preschool Chain.

2023

Learning Beyond the Classroom

Launched the Web App, expanding learning beyond the classroom. Earned the esteemed title of India's Most Trusted Brand.

2024

Embracing Fresh Milestones

Garnered titles of India's Most Trusted Preschool and India's Most Trusted Preschool. Beyond borders, Sanford Junior stands as a beacon of trust and excellence.
