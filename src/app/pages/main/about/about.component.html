ABOUT SANFORD

SANFORD – Group Of Schools is a brainchild of husband-wife duo M. K. Meena& Mamta Meena. They laid the foundation of their first preschool in 2010 with a bouquet of attraction, concepts, and role-play models for tiny tots based on the ‘play-way’ methodology or ‘learning by doing. After more than 10 years of rich experience in the field, they launched their school brand SANFORD in December 2018 based on the U. K. Concept of preschool education.

SANFORD curriculum involves effective, time-tested elements of traditional learning infused with cutting-edge technology resources, which bridges the learning gap. The curriculum is crafted in the hexagram of ‘Core Value Circle’ which helps children in the Refinement of their senses, Gross Motor Development, Creative Development, Social Development, Psychological/ Personality Development, and Practical life care for Self, Others, and the Environment. The classrooms are set up with world-class technology-enabled teaching tools to make teaching more interesting and effective. Children play on the large touch panels with the specially designed software and simultaneously learn alphabets, numbers, shapes, and many more. The children are taken in an ordered manner through the day to be able to give them a sense of routine to help them settle in a new environment in prospect.

OUR MISSION

SANFORD’s mission is to provide an enriching learning experience to the early learners in their first steps towards education.

We strive to create a tranquil, curious and challenging environment that fosters the social, physical, emotional and cognitive development of the child.

Our aim is to imbibe social, moral and cultural values in early learners and also to arouse in them the feeling of respect and concern towards the environment.

OUR VISSION

t’s our vision to nurture today’s learners into tomorrow’s responsible and passionate global citizens, as these children are going to shape the future of our society tomorrow.

GLOBAL ALLIANCE

Trinity College Of London

SANFORD is the pioneer preschool chain partnered with TRINITY COLLEGE, LONDON for The Trinity Stars: Young Performers in English Award, designed to encourage the teaching and learning of English language through drama, music and performance for children who are starting to learn English and to offer them an international Certificate at this early age. Preparing children for the award shows how performance-related activity support learning, motivates children and builds their confidence and communication skills. Trinity Stars has been designed to support both learning in the classroom and teachers’ professional development.

British Association Of Early Childhood Education

SANFORD is Member of “British Association of Early Childhood Education” – U.K. This helps SANFORD WORLD getting regular updates on the innovation in early education and trainings from both of these organizations time to time.
